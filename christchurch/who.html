About

Every year, more than 1 million people choose Cover•More travel insurance. For more than 25 years, Cover•More has been providing travellers in New Zealand, Australia and the UK with high-quality cover and round-the clock customer service.

Although we manage millions of policies and thousands of claims each year, we treat each customer with the individual care they deserve. With more than 200 travel insurance and emergency specialists in our employ, we are able to meet our customer's needs quickly, thoroughly, and effectively–anywhere, anytime.

Frequently Asked Questions

Cover

  • How can I be sure I’m choosing quality cover?

    There are many things to look out for when choosing a travel insurance provider and although it may appear that some policies are very similar, quality cover is more than just high policy limits. Make sure you compare plans by looking into the sub limits and carefully reading the policy exclusions.

    • With Cover-More, our policies don’t just cover your luggage, they also provide cover for the costs of you having to unexpectedly cancel or amend your trip, accommodation and medical costs should you become ill or get injured, experience a travel delay, have your luggage lost or stolen and much more.
    • Most importantly, you will have access to our 24 hour emergency assistance team so help is just a phone call away 24 hours a day, 365 days of the year.
    • When you compare travel insurance policies ensure you look closely at what cover you’ll have for major world disasters. You will soon discover that we cover what many others don't—including medical costs for injuries resulting from terrorism attacks, civil unrest, strikes, natural disasters such as earthquakes and volcanic eruptions, redundancy, working holidays, and unlimited dental.

    Please read your Policy Brochure for full details of the cover provided.

  • Will you cover me for a one-way trip?

    Yes in most cases we will, with Options or Essentials up to 12 months. Please contact your Travel Agent or our Customer Service Department for more information.

  • Can I purchase an Annual 'Business' policy?

    Yes, if you meet the following criteria:

    • You are up to 69 years of age at the time of policy issue
    • You are a New Zealand resident
    • You are travelling mainly for business purposes and for a New Zealand company

    You may find that an Annual Multi-Trip policy may suit you if you do not fit these criteria.

  • I'm going overseas for over a year, will you cover me?

    In most cases yes we will. Cover-More Travel Insurance policies allow you to stay away for a maximum of 24 months. There are tighter restrictions on durations for some destinations and age groups. In particular, if you are travelling to the Americas or Africa, we can only offer cover up to 18months. Please contact your Travel Agent or our Customer Service Department for more details.

  • What do I do if my luggage is delayed?

    There is provision to claim for the purchase of essential items of clothing and toiletries if your entire luggage is delayed for more than 24 hours by a Transport Provider. Limits of this cover on:

    • Options (Plan I) are up to $500 for delays in excess of 24 hours and up to $2000 for delays in excess of 72 hours
    • Essentials (Plan IE) is up to $500 if delay is in excess of 24 hours
    • Business is up to $500 for delays in excess of 24 hours and up to $1000 for delays in excess of 48 hours.

    Corporate is up to $1000 for delays in excess of 12 hours and up to $2000 for delays in excess of 72 hours

  • How many accompanied children are allowed to travel on a policy?

    We automatically cover your children and grandchildren plus 1 non-related child per adult traveller provided they are all dependent (not in full time employment) and under 21 years. (Please Note: Children are not covered on any Domestic Plan C policies (Cancellation and Amendment for Domestic) – on this plan the premium is per person.

    Corporate Plans provide cover for six accompanying children to travel on the Corporate policy as long as they are travelling with the policyholder.

  • I will be Snow Skiing/Snowboarding, am I covered?

    Yes. Cover-More Travel Insurance policies offer cover for snow skiing and snowboarding provided you pay an additional premium. However you will NOT be covered if you are racing, off-piste or participating in a professional capacity even if you pay the extra amount. Get more details about ski travel insurance.

  • I am taking part in a race or sporting competition. Am I covered?

    We cover many different types of sporting activities as long as the activity is not in a professional capacity and not listed in the "We Will Not Under Any Section Pay For" section of the policy wording. We cannot provide cover for participation in any races other than on foot (i.e. walking or running).

  • Can I get extra coverage for a valuable item?

    Certainly! You may add extra cover for valuables at the time of purchase, or any time before your departure. Check your policy brochure for limits. Please note that you will need to provide us with a valuation and the purchase receipt for the items if you need to claim on an item.
