Thank You.

Your travel insurance claim has been submitted.

You will receive a confirmation email containing your claim number accompanied by a claim summary.  You can login and check your claim status anytime using your name, date of birth and policy number.

We will begin to assess your claim and reply to you with a progress update within 10 business days, however if you have indicated that you will be providing some documents at a later date, we will wait to hear from you before assessing your claim in full.

Remember you can log in to our claims centre at any time to:

  • Check the progress of your claim
  • Attach additional supporting documentation
  • Submit another claim.

Important: if you have not submitted all of your supporting documentation then we will not be able to completely assess your claim.

If you haven’t uploaded all of your supporting documents, you can return to the home screen and correct any missing items online.

You can also email them to us at claims_processing@covermore.com.au or fax them to 02 9202 8098.

For faster processing, we recommend completing your submission online.

What to expect after submitting a claim

  1. We will respond with details regarding your claim and its progress within 10 business days.
  2. If you have indicated that you will be sending more documents to support your claim soon, we will await their submission before responding to you.
  3. If information was missing when you submitted your claim, we will send you an email explaining what is still required. You can log back in to our claims centre to upload any additional supporting documents.
  4. Once we have received everything, we will be in contact to inform you we will be proceeding with the processing of your claim.
